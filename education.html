Higher Secondary Schools

Chhoo Lo Aasman

"Chhoo Lo Aasman" in Dantewada, Chhattisgarh, is a pioneering program focused on providing
quality education and holistic development to underprivileged children from remote and
marginalized communities. This residential schooling initiative offers state-of-the-art
infrastructure, qualified faculty, and comprehensive facilities for academics, sports, and
extracurricular activities. By emphasizing vocational training and skill development, it aims to
prepare students for future careers. The program has significantly increased school enrollment and
improved academic performance, with a special focus on empowering girls through education.
"Chhoo Lo Aasman" is a beacon of hope, helping children reach their full potential and achieve their dreams.

Jawahar Navodaya

Navodaya Vidyalaya in Barsur, Chhattisgarh, is part of the nationwide Navodaya Vidyalaya network, which aims
to provide quality education to talented rural children. This co-educational residential school focuses on holistic
development, offering modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and comprehensive facilities for academics, sports,
and extracurricular activities. By nurturing students' potential and fostering a spirit of excellence, Navodaya Vidyalaya,
Barsur, plays a crucial role in shaping future leaders and contributing to the educational upliftment of the region.

Handicraft Student School

Saksham Vidyalay

Saksham Vidyalaya in Jawanga, Chhattisgarh, specializes in providing education focused on handicrafts. The school aims to
preserve and promote traditional craftsmanship while offering quality education. Students receive training in various handicraft
skills alongside their regular academic curriculum, ensuring holistic development. With modern infrastructure and skilled instructors,
Saksham Vidyalaya fosters a nurturing environment where students can hone their artistic talents and prepare for future careers in the
handicraft industry. The school plays a vital role in empowering local youth and preserving cultural heritage through education Watch Video
