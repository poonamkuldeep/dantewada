Dantewada's livelihood is predominantly based on agriculture, mining, and handicrafts.
The NMDC's presence has boosted local employment, while agricultural activities, including
rice and millet farming, remain crucial. Handicrafts, particularly those made by local tribes,
are gaining recognition and providing additional income sources for many families.
